Count on

This is the most basic method of counting. It involves picking the largest number and literaly counting from there

E.g. 5 + 3 = ? Well 5 + 1 = 6 5 + 2 = 7 5 + 3 = 8!

Or b + 4 = ? Well b + 1 = c b + 2 = d b + 3 = e b + 4 = f!

Counting on your fingers is the easiest way to use this method. For systems more then base 10, you do get edge cases whereyou run out of finger (5+b!) But there's not many of them, and a numberline can help a lot.
